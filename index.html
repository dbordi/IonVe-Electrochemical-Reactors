Long-duration storage is economically viable for industry today

Industrial sites with high demand charges and time-of-use contracts already generate a positive business case for 4–12 hour storage. The barrier is not economics — it is the absence of an affordable, reliable product.

02

Li-ion is not cost-effective beyond 4–6 hours

At durations of 6 hours or more, the 30-year total cost of ownership of Li-ion is significantly higher than VRFB, due to battery replacements every 10–12 years.

Where storage matters now

Variable renewables dominate new generation. The next bottlenecks are flexibility, safety and life‑cycle cost. Long‑duration, safe storage smooths variability, avoids curtailment and unlocks new business models.

Application areas for redox‑flow storage

  • PV self‑consumption (BTM): store daytime solar, dispatch later to cut grid purchases and flatten peaks.
  • Price arbitrage & congestion: shift energy to high‑value hours and ride through grid constraints with cycle‑tolerant storage.
  • Co‑location with wind & solar: on‑site storage minimizes transport losses and scales modularly.
  • Utility support: peak shaving, ramp‑rate control and capacity firming at grid bottlenecks.

Why redox flow batteries?

RFBs circulate liquid electrolytes through electrochemical cells. Energy (tank volume) and power (cell area/stack count) are decoupled, enabling tailored duration at constant stack design. Aqueous chemistry, deep cycling and easy refurbishment deliver long life, high safety and stable performance.

4 Architecture Innovations — engineered for cost, efficiency & scale

🧩
Conductive Polymer Frames — a single mouldable part integrates structural, electrical and sealing functions, replacing bipolar plate + flow frame + gasket. Enables injection & compression moulding at industrial volume.
⚡
High Power Microstack — Modular Architecture — standardised 10-cell modules (ministacks) with defined hydraulic & electrical interfaces. Hot-swap maintenance, automatable assembly. Target: 32–35% stack cost reduction, 3.4× fewer units at equivalent power.
💧
Selective Hydraulic Distribution — per-module manifolding eliminates shared electrolyte paths, suppressing parasitic shunt currents and reducing pressure drop. Estimated: +5 pp round-trip efficiency.
🏭
IonVe VRFB System Platform — all innovations integrated into a single, repeatable industrial product: cell design, stack architecture, hydraulics, electronics and control in one co-designed system.
